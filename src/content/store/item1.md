## Project Overview
After a long semester, planning a trip can be overwhelming. Our AI-powered platform simplifies the process by handling everything from itinerary creation to budget estimation and real-time weather updates. Users can input their destination, and the system takes care of the logistics, ensuring a stress-free experience.


## Features

![Itinerary Example](/Jatra_features.png)

### Basic Itinerary Generation
- Enter a destination, and get a suggested itinerary.
- Includes transport options, estimated travel time, meal plans, and accommodation recommendations.
- Budget estimation with cost breakdown.


### Interactive Map Integration
- View key locations like hotels, restaurants, and transport options on a dynamic map.
- Interactive pins with detailed information.
- Route visualization between stops.


### Real-time Weather Notifications
- Fetch weather updates for trip dates and locations.
- Alerts for rain, storms, or extreme temperatures.
- Adaptive recommendations based on weather conditions.


### AI-Generated Trip Blog
- Automatically generates a travel blog post after the trip.
- Highlights memorable events and locations.
- Personalized recap based on user activity.


### Image Upload & Smart Search
- Upload trip photos and organize them into albums.
- AI-powered textual search to find images using natural language queries.
- Enhanced search accuracy for memorable moments.


![Photo Search](/Jatra_workflow.png)

## How It Works
1. Enter your destination and preferences.
2. Get a personalized itinerary with real-time updates.
3. Enjoy your trip with live weather alerts and navigation support.
4. Relive your journey with a custom travel blog and searchable photos.
